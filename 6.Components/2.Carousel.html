Pipe Wrench

The adjustable pipe, or Stillson, wrench is used to hold or turn pipes or circular bars.

Wrench

Wrenches are made in various shapes and sizes and are used for gripping, fastening, turning, tightening and loosening things like pipes, pipe fittings, nuts and bolts.

Ratchet

Mechanical tool that tightens and loosens nuts and bolts more efficiently than other wrenches.

Screws

The most common use of screws is to hold things together and position items.
